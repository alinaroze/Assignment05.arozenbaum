Navigating Google Books

This is a quick and easy how-to on navigating Google Books.

For this tutorial you will need a Google account and be logged in to it. Instructions on how to create a Google account can be found here .

Go to Google Books.

Finding and Adding a Book

  • Type in the name of a book you would like to find.
  • Several book links will pop-up. Click on one.
  • Near the top of the page, right under the search bar, will be a button: Add to my library. Hover over it.
  • Now you can either click on one of the book shelves to add it to that one, or you can look at the left side bar of the page.

Finding and Adding Several Books

  • Click on what says: My library.
  • You should now be on a different page.
  • On the left side bar there is a list of bookshelves that are currently in your library.
  • Click on one.
  • Now click on the Settings on your right.
  • One of the options should be: Add by ISBN or ISSN. Click on it
  • Now, if you know the ISBN/ISSN of your books you can add them to this shelf by following the example provided.

Creating a Bookshelf

  • Click on what says: My library.
  • You should now be on a different page.
  • On the left side bar there is a list of bookshelves that are currently in your library, or you can add a new one by clicking: NEW SHELF.
  • Now just type in the name and specify your preferences.
  • Click: Create bookshelf.
  • Now you are free to add books to it!

Exporting a Bookshelf to XMl File

  • Using the above directions add Add 15 of your favorite books, as well as 10 books from the NYTimes 2014 bestseller list (post April 1, 2014).
  • Now click on the Settings on your right.
  • One of the options should be: Export as XML. Click on it
  • Simply proceed to save to the file you need it to be in.
