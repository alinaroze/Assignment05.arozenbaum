Retrieving an NY Times Bestsellers List API

Here I will be showing you how to retrieve an API from NY Times so that you can use it in your Yahoo Pipe and get up-to-date information on the bestsellers in the hard-cover fiction section of the NY Times.

Go to the NY Times Developers page.

  • Click on the Register button in the top right hand corner.
  • Follow the steps and fill out the form to create an account in The New York Times.
  • Click on Create my account.
  • Now go to NY Times Developers Application page.
  • Fill out the registration form, remembering to click the check box further down next to; Issue a new key for Bestsellers API.
  • Finally click the check bo next to; I agree to the terms of service .
  • Click Register Application.
  • Now either copy and paste the key displayed into a wordDoc and save, or simply keep the e-mail that was sent to you through the e-mail address you provided.

Now that you have your key, simply use the following URL template, substituting your key where it says: {KEY}. Also, change the colons to percent signs: % inside the API key.

http://api.nytimes.com/svc/books/v2/lists.xml?api-key={KEY} &list=hardcover-fiction
